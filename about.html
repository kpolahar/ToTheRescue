Meet Kathy

To The Rescue CPR is a family-operated business. Owner Katherine Johnson is certified by the American Heart Association and the Emergency Care and Safety Institute.

Kathy has worked in the medical field for 25 years and enjoys teaching a wide variety of classes to the community. She teaches classes for all ages and skill levels, from toddlers to medical professionals.

Our Mission

At To The Rescue CPR, we believe that everyone has the right to receive life-saving training at an affordable cost. Our classes are taught in a relaxing and stress-free environment at our training center (on-site training offered for groups 8+). We will work with you until you have the knowledge and confidence to go into the community prepared to help in a medical or cardiac emergency.
